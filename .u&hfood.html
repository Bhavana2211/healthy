HEALTHY FOOD&UNHEALTHY FOOD

healthy food

healthy food - A food item rich in nutritional value that benefits the overall well being of your body functions can be referred to ashealthy food Four main food groups make a balanced healthy diet:
  1. Vegetables and Fruits
  1. Meat
  1. Whole grains
  1. Dairy
These food items must be included in your diet with the correct preparation methods. The right preparation method will ensure you get the necessary nutrients, such as carbohydrates, proteins, vitamins and minerals, in the correct proportion. The extent and type of nutrients required will be based on your age, pre-existing illness, or other health issues.

unhealthy food

unhealthy food- Food items that are less in the necessary nutritional value and have increased bad fat, calories, etc., can be referred to as unhealthy food. Processed food items, fast foods, fried food items, etc., are unhealthy foods. All fats are not bad. Therefore, it is important to know the difference between good fats and bad fats. polyunsaturated and monounsaturated fats affect the cholesterol levels positively in your body. They increase the good cholesterol and decrease the bad cholesterol levels referred to as the HDL - High-Density Lipoprotein and the LDL -Low-Density Lipoprotein, respectively. On the other hand, saturated fat derived from full-fat dairy products, red meat, etc., and the trans fat from food items like microwave pizza, popcorn and baked items like cake, cookies, etc., are unhealthy fats.
